Welcome to Pa Gya! 2021

About the Festival

Brought to you by Writers Project of Ghana and Goethe-Institut Ghana, the 2020 edition of Pa Gya! will feature readings, panel discussions, poetry performances, book launches, literary prize awards, and so on. Click on the tabs below for links to authors, events, and the schedule.

Pa Gya! will be held at the Goethe-Institut, Cantonments, Accra, Ghana. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, events during this year's festival will have limited seating as allowed by law.

All events will also be available live online via this website.
