The Writers Project Book Club Show

January 1, 2021

Writers Project of Ghana is happy to introduce a new program, the "Writers Project Book Club Show", a monthly book discussion session which will be streamed online.

The programme features a selected book of the month. At the end of each month, invited guests will join a panel to discuss and review the book.

The "Writers Project Book Club Show" seeks to increase interest in books by bringing together readers for an hour of engaging conversation. The show will highlight Ghanaian literature and literature by Africans and the Black Diaspora.

The Abena Korantemaa Oral History Prize (2020)

December 23, 2020

Writers Project of Ghana announces the opening of submissions for the 2020 Abena Korantemaa Oral History Prize, an annual award for Oral History narrated by a woman over the age of 65 years.

The Abena Korantemaa Oral History Prize is set up to encourage younger Ghanaians to document history and contribute to its preservation. The prize also seeks to encourage the production of creative non-fiction from Ghana. This prize was established by the family of Abena Korantemaa, and is sponsored by MAKEDA PR and Sankofa Girl.

The competition is open from now until 18th September, 2020. The prizes will be awarded in October, 2020, and the first prize is GHC 2,500.
